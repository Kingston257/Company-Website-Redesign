Procurement & Supply Chain

Fedoz represents some of the most reliable OEMs for various kinds of equipment and devices utilized in the energy industry, such as, pumps and their accessories (mechanical seals, gaskets, couplings, sleeves etc.), rotating equipment, a full range of valves (including HIPPS), pipes & fittings, instruments, anti-theft locks, and more.
Beyond our OEM partnerships, we leverage on our global supply chain network to source and supply other materials that are not directly covered by our OEMs.
Additionally, we also have a strong network of international shipping, freight handling, & clearing agencies to expedite our procurement operations
