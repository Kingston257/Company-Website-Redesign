Oil & Gas Facility Maintenance and Asset Management

Fedoz prides itself as one of Nigeria’s indigenous companies with strong capacity in oil & gas equipment maintenance and management, leveraging on the technical support of our OEMs and our vast supply chain network for sourcing of parts.
Our core equipment maintenance services include;
- Pumps Servicing and Restoration
- HV & LV Electric Motor Servicing, Rewinding & Restoration
- Valves Servicing
- Instrument Servicing and Calibration
- HVAC Servicing
- Plantwide Asset Management, Retrofitting and Reverse Engineering

Our Maintenance Workshop and Materials Warehouse stock critical spares for maintenance services.
We also retain constant OEM collaboration and support, allowing us access to best quality spares at shorter lead-times.
