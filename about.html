About Us

Business Model

Fedoz Nigeria Limited (FNL) operates across various sectors including Oil & Gas, Energy, Chemical, Water, Power, FMCG’s, and delivering engineering services seamlessly for the past 20 years.

Our main business areas are:
- Facilities Operations & Maintenance (O&M)/Assets Management
- EPIC Projects Delivery
- Manpower Supply Services

FNL is at the forefront of technological innovation, leveraging cutting-edge solutions to improve operational efficiency and customer experience. 

Commitment to Engineering Excellence, Safety & Quality

At Fedoz Nigeria Limited, we uphold the highest engineering performance & QHSE standards, ensuring safety and excellence in every project. Our COREN registration and ISO 45001:2018 certification reflects our commitment to a zero-harm workplace and proactive hazard prevention.

Through rigorous technical processes, quality control, continuous safety training, and environmental sustainability practices, we mitigate risks and enhance operational efficiency— delivering solutions that prioritize both safety and excellence.

Vision for the future

We envision a future where FNL is recognized as a leading global Oil & Gas servicing company, known for its efficient and quality service delivery and commitment to social responsibility. 

Core Values

Our core values of Integrity, Respect, and People guide our actions and decisions, ensuring that we maintain a strong ethical foundation and a culture of continuous improvement. 

Local Content

At Fedoz Nigeria Limited, we are committed to fostering local
content development as a core pillar of our sustainability
strategy. We fully comply with the Nigerian Content
Development and Monitoring Board (NCDMB) regulations,
ensuring that Nigerian goods, services, and personnel are
prioritized in our operations. Through trainings and capacity
development, we contribute to the growth of Nigeria’s local
content in the oil and gas industry while delivering world-class
solutions.

Strategic Partnerships

World-class collaborations -
We have established strategic partnerships with local and international entities, enhancing our reach and capabilities in various industries.

Sustainability Initiatives

We are committed to sustainability, implementing eco-friendly practices and initiatives to minimize environmental carbon footprint and contribute to a greener future.
