Services

Oil & Gas Facilities Maintenance & Asset Management

Fedoz has strong capacity in Oil & Gas equipment maintenance and management, leveraging on the technical support of our OEMs and our vast supply chain network for sourcing of parts. Our core equipment maintenance services include; Pumps, HV & LV Electric Motors, Valves & HVAC Servicing, Instrument servicing and calibration, General plantwide asset maintenance and fabrication. 

Engineering, Procurement, Installation & Construction (EPIC) Projects Execution

FNL executes a wide range of EPIC projects, starting with the Design Engineering phase to the commissioning. Our EPIC and Fabrication Project execution is supported by experienced QAQC and Safety teams to ensure Quality and Safety are not compromised...

Procurement & Supply Chain

Fedoz represents some of the most reliable OEMs for different kinds of equipment and devices utilized in the industry, such as pumps and their accessories (mechanical seals, gaskets, couplings, sleeves etc.), rotating equipment, a full range of valves (including HIPPS), pipes & fittings, instruments, anti-theft locks, and more...  

Manpower Supply

We provide manpower supply services for a broad spectrum of service needs: Assets Management, Project Management and Supervision, Operations and Maintenance, Training etc. We source our manpower personnel needs from some of the best recruitment companies locally and international.
